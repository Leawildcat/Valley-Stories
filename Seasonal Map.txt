# **🌱 EARLY SPRING — “The Thawing”**  
**Late March – Early May**

**Natural world:**  
- Snowmelt feeds the river  
- First shoots appear  
- Moss brightens  
- Fog is common in mornings  
- Thimbleberries begin forming but aren’t ripe yet  
- Migratory birds return  
- The forest feels watchful but gentle  

**Human/Valley rhythms:**  
- Lea begins foraging early greens  
- First rituals of renewal  
- The brothers entering the forest here feels symbolic — the Valley is waking  
- Paths are muddy, rivers high  
- Spirits/old things are quiet but stirring  

**Harvests:**  
- Nettles  
- Wild greens  
- Early herbs  
- Thimbleberry blossoms  

---

# **🌸 LATE SPRING — “The First Fruits”**  
**Mid May – Early June**

**Natural world:**  
- Thimbleberries ripen **now**  
- Bees everywhere  
- Warm days, cool nights  
- The forest canopy fills in  
- Waterfalls strongest  

**Human/Valley rhythms:**  
- Lea harvests thimbleberries  
- Wine making begins  
- She might make 4–8 bottles depending on the year  
- This is when she’s busiest in the woods  
- The Valley feels alive, humming, almost too awake  

**Harvests:**  
- Thimbleberries  
- Early flowers  
- Wild mint  
- Morel mushrooms  

**Wine timeline:**  
- Drinkable by late summer  
- Good by fall  
- Excellent by winter  
- One bottle left by next spring? Totally normal  

---

# **🍒 EARLY SUMMER — “The Red Season”**  
**Mid June – Early July**

**Natural world:**  
- Cherries ripen **now**  
- Heat begins building  
- Long golden evenings  
- Fireflies  
- River calmer  
- Forest thick, lush, almost claustrophobic  

**Human/Valley rhythms:**  
- Frank arriving now is PERFECT  
- Lea is in peak foraging mode  
- Rituals of abundance  
- Nights feel heavy and enchanted  
- This is when the Valley feels most magical and most dangerous  

**Harvests:**  
- Cherries  
- Wild strawberries  
- Early plums  
- Herbs at peak potency  

---

# **🔥 HIGH SUMMER — “The Heat”**  
**July – August**

**Natural world:**  
- Heat haze  
- Afternoon storms  
- Forest dense and shadowed  
- Animals move at dawn/dusk  
- Water levels drop  

**Human/Valley rhythms:**  
- Lea stays near water  
- Wine ferments quietly  
- This is the season when old magic sleeps lightly  
- Nights are loud with insects  
- Tempers run short, emotions run high  

**Harvests:**  
- Blackberries  
- Wildflowers  
- Medicinal herbs  

---

# **🍂 EARLY FALL — “The Gathering”**  
**September – Early October**

**Natural world:**  
- Leaves begin turning  
- Crisp mornings  
- Mushrooms everywhere  
- Animals preparing for winter  

**Human/Valley rhythms:**  
- Lea finishes preserving  
- Wine from spring is now **excellent**  
- This is when she’d share a bottle with someone meaningful  
- The Valley feels wise, old, and a little sad  

**Harvests:**  
- Apples  
- Late berries  
- Mushrooms  
- Nuts  

---

# **🍁 LATE FALL — “The Quieting”**  
**Late October – November**

**Natural world:**  
- Heavy fog  
- First frost  
- Forest goes silent  
- Winds pick up  

**Human/Valley rhythms:**  
- Lea withdraws  
- Rituals of remembrance  
- The Valley feels thin, liminal  
- Spirits/old things are closest now  

**Harvests:**  
- Roots  
- Last herbs  
- Final apples  

---

# **❄️ WINTER — “The Deeping”**  
**December – February**

**Natural world:**  
- Snow  
- Ice  
- Stillness  
- Long nights  
- Stars sharp and bright  

**Human/Valley rhythms:**  
- Lea stays home  
- Drinks last year’s wine  
- Repairs tools  
- Dreams vividly  
- The Valley sleeps but dreams loudly  

**Harvests:**  
- None  
- Only what was stored
